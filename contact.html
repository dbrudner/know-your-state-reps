Our purpose is to increase voter awareness of state representatives. Tools like this exist at the federal level in spades, but there is nothing like this for state governments. The majority of voters don't even know the names of their state reps, let alone how they actually represent them in state legislatures.


Our goal is to help voters stay in the know and help you make mindful, informed decisions in elections.
