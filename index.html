Do you know who your state representatives are?
What about their voting record?

Most Americans don't know who is representing them, much less at the state and local level where most governing occurs.

To get started, click the crosshairs location_searching to automatically detect your location or type in your address and search search for your reps. Your reps will display in the tiles below. Click on a tile to get detailed information about each rep, including their voting record.

If you'd like information on a particular bill, click on the menu button menu in the top right corner and head on over to the advanced bill search page.
